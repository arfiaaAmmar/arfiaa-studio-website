Who is Arfiaa Ammar ?

My name is Ammar Hazim aka Arfiaa Ammar. I'm a professional photographer based in Perak, Malaysia. I am primarily known for my portrait, events and wedding photography work in Perak and Shah Alam area. Whether I'm documenting my personal life or working for my clients, I'm striving to create the best photos possible.
